能帮我一下吗？
Would you mind opening the window?
你介意开窗吗？
I was wondering if you could help.

询问信息

到那里要多久？
Do you know where the station is?
你知道车站在哪吗？
Can you tell me how to get to the museum?

购物表达
How much is this? Is it on sale?
这个多少钱？打折吗？
Can I try this on? Do you have a larger size?
能试穿吗？有更大号吗？
Can I get a refund if it doesn't fit?

我要烤鸡。
What do you recommend? Any specials?

I am allergic to nuts. Does this contain any?

I need help immediately. Please call an ambulance.

I have lost my passport. My wallet has been stolen.
