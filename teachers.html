Our belief

"A spring does not decide to flow. It simply does, because that is what it is."

We built Well Talk around the quiet conviction that the best teaching comes from a place that never runs dry. Not from scripts. Not from dashboards that measure how often you smile. Not from parents treating you like a talking toy for their child. Just the steady, renewable craft of a teacher who still loves what they do — and is given the conditions to keep loving it.

The way it is

You audition every day.
Platforms score your "energy level." Parents watch the replay and complain about your background. You trained to teach, not to perform. But somewhere along the way, teaching became entertainment, and you became the show.
You never see the same student twice.
Kids booked by the hour. Trial classes that lead nowhere. A different face on every call. You cannot build a relationship in 25 minutes. You cannot measure growth when the student disappears after three sessions.
The platform takes half. You do all the work.
You prep, you teach, you write feedback, you manage the parent. The platform provides a Zoom link and takes 30-50% of every dollar. That math has never made sense.

How we do it

We recruit. You teach.
We find the students. We provide a structured KET-to-PET curriculum built by experienced teachers — not generated by an algorithm. You adapt it to your style. That is the full job description.
Long-term students. Real progress.
Students are placed on a clear learning path and stay with the same teacher for months, often over a year. You see them grow. You build trust. You become part of their story — not a disposable session in their app history.
Fair pay. Transparent numbers.
We are a small team, not a venture-funded platform burning cash on ads. You take home the vast majority of what your students pay. We show you the breakdown. No hidden fees. No surprise deductions.

Who we are looking for

You still believe in this.

1.You know the difference between teaching and entertaining — and you chose teaching for a reason.
2.You want consistency. A steady group of students you actually get to know over months, not a revolving door of trial classes.
3.You speak English natively or at a near-native level. Accent is irrelevant. Clarity, warmth, and the ability to listen are everything.
4.You value your autonomy. You want structure but not surveillance. Guidance but not a script.
5.You are looking for a home, not a gig. A place where your work accumulates meaning over time.
